Questions

Frequently asked

Short answers to the questions we hear most. The full FAQ has more.

Is TAGRA cloud-based or installed locally?
TAGRA is Windows desktop software that runs entirely offline on your own computer. Driver and vehicle data never leaves your network — there is no cloud upload, no third-party processor, and no monthly subscription. Many fleets prefer this for GDPR and data-protection reasons.
Does TAGRA cover EU drivers' hours and the Working Time Directive?
Yes. TAGRA evaluates Regulation (EC) No 561/2006 (EU drivers' hours) and the HGV Working Time Directive 2002/15/EC, plus related AETR rules. Every infringement is highlighted in clear driver and vehicle reports with exact time, type and duration.
Which tachographs and card readers does it support?
All EU digital tachographs, from first-generation through Smart Tachograph G2V2. Compatible with standard smart-card readers (e.g. DOWNLOAD BOX II S) and reads .DDD, .C1B, .V1B and ESM files. Ready for the July 2026 requirements for light commercial vehicles.
How does the 30-day free trial work?
Download and install the Windows package — you get 30 days of full, unrestricted access to all features. No credit card, no payment information. If TAGRA fits your operation, you purchase a perpetual licence to continue.
